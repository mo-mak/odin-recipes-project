Cacio e Pepe

Description

Cacio e Pepe is one of those dishes that proves the power of simple ingredients. This pasta is made using a combination of 6 simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pepper
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Bring water and pasta to a boil
  2. Rinse pasta and save a little bit of the water
  3. Mince garlic and fry it in olive oil
  4. Chop up your parsely
  5. Drop pasta into the pan once garlic turns golden brown
  6. Mix pasta, pasta water, garlic, minces parsley, lemon juice, and salt and pepper all togethr
  7. Turn off heat after a few minutes, serve in a good bowl and garnish with lots of cheese
