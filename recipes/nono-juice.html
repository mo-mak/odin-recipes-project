No No Juice

Description

No No juice gets its name from "No, honey, you can't drink that, that's no no juice". It's wine; No No Juice is wine.

Ingredients

  • Red or white Wine
  • Wine glass
  • Wine opener

Steps

  1. Open wine
  2. Grab wine bottle
  3. Place wine glass on table or hold glass in hand
  4. Pour desired amount of wine into glass
  5. Refer to wine as no no juice if there are kids nearby
